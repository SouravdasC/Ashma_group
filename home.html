WELCOME TO ASHMA GROUP OF COMPANIES

WELCOME TO JALAN GROUP ASHMA GROUP OF COMPANIES, with its Head Office based in Kolkata, is home to a highly experienced team of logistics and industrial estate professionals. Our unwavering commitment to providing top-notch services extends across India. By combining global developments and expertise with dedicated capital, we strive to create critical industrial and logistic infrastructure essential for India’s rapid economic growth. One of our core values lies in achieving goals in a timely manner while continuously improving the infrastructure sector. Our group takes immense pride in adapting to the evolving industry landscape and catering to diverse project requirements. By actively contributing to developing lands, warehouses, industrial parks, commercial shops, roads & infrastructure, we play a vital role in bolstering economic growth and facilitating a conducive environment for businesses to thrive.
