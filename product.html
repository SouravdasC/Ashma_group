ASHMA construction Specifications

Construction: A Medium Description Construction is the process of building, assembling, or modifying structures such as houses, roads, bridges, and commercial buildings. It involves various stages, including planning, designing, material selection, and execution. The construction industry is crucial for infrastructure development and economic growth.
Key Phases of Construction :
Planning & Design – Architects and engineers create blueprints and structural designs. Site Preparation – Clearing land, leveling, and laying foundations. Structural Work – Erecting frameworks, walls, roofing, and concrete structures. Finishing & Utilities – Installing plumbing, electrical systems, and interior finishing. Inspection & Handover – Quality checks ensure safety before final handover.
Types of Construction :
Residential – Houses, apartments, and townships. Commercial – Office buildings, malls, and hotels. Infrastructure – Roads, bridges, railways, and tunnels. Industrial – Factories, warehouses, and power plants.
