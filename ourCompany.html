about us

Jalan Group has been a pioneering force in modern industrial and warehouse development in India since 1982 by visionary entrepreneurs. Guided by the leadership of Mr Ajay Mohan Jalan, Chairman of the company, it rapidly gained momentum and grew from strength to strength and expanded into the Industrial Park, Warehouse and Infrastructure development.

We take pride in its diverse range of services, which include industrial parks, warehouses, roads, and infrastructure. These offerings cater to the evolving needs of businesses in the industrial and logistics sectors. By providing state-of-the-art facilities and comprehensive support, we aim to empower our clients and foster their growth. We have successfully transformed the industrial landscape of the eastern states, particularly in Howrah, by strategically developing projects along NH 6. Our flagship project, the JALAN INDUSTRIAL COMPLEX, offers prime location advantages and world-class infrastructure.

Driven by a strong belief in growth and innovation, we expanded our operations exponentially over four decades. The remarkable achievement underscores our company’s commitment to continuous advancement and its unwavering focus on excellence in the road and highway infrastructure. Jalan Group of Industries is making a mark in the Infrastructure Sector by creating its new wing in the name of JALAN INFRASTRUCTURE LLP.
